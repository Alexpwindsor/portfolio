About me

Design that moves
the needle

Based in San Francisco, I obsess over how people interact with technology at scale. From shaping Zelle at Wells Fargo to defining QuickBooks' visual language at Intuit — I bridge design craft with business outcomes.

I lead cross-functional teams, manage design programs end-to-end, and bring a rare combination of strategic vision and hands-on execution. My work has earned 4 US patents, a best-in-class network rating, and measurable NPS growth at one of the largest banks in the country.
